## Next Steps for Maximum Traffic

### Immediate Actions
1. **Submit to Google Search Console**
   - Go to: https://search.google.com/search-console/
   - Add your domain and submit sitemap.xml
   - Verify ownership

2. **Submit to Google My Business**
   - Go to: https://www.google.com/business/
   - Create/claim your business listing
   - Add photos, hours, services

3. **Update Contact Information**
   - Replace generic contact info with actual business details
   - Verify phone number and email work

### Content Strategy
1. **Blog Optimization**
   - Blog posts should be 800-1200 words minimum
   - Use internal links to connect related posts
   - Include keywords naturally in content
   - Add images with descriptive alt text

2. **Service Pages** (Optional enhancement)
   - Create dedicated pages for each service:
     - `/printing-services.html`
     - `/kra-tax-filing.html`
     - `/business-registration.html`
     - `/graphic-design.html`
   - Each page targets specific keywords

### Technical SEO
1. **Page Speed**
   - Compress images (use tools like tinypng.com)
   - Minify CSS and JavaScript
   - Use a CDN for faster delivery

2. **Mobile Optimization**
   - Test on mobile: https://search.google.com/test/mobile-friendly
   - Ensure buttons and links are touch-friendly
   - Use responsive design (your site already does this)

3. **SSL Certificate**
   - Use HTTPS (https://www.skyteeycomputers.com)
   - This is now a ranking factor for Google

### Link Building & Authority
1. **Local Directories**
   - List your business on:
     - Google My Business
     - Yelp (if applicable)
     - Local Nairobi business directories
     - Kenya business listings

2. **Backlinks**
   - Get links from local Nairobi websites
   - Contribute to tech/printing industry blogs
   - Partner with complementary businesses

### Social Media Integration
1. Add social media sharing buttons to blog posts
2. Share blog content on Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp
3. Encourage customer reviews on Google My Business
4. Link social profiles in footer/header
